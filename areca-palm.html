Description

It is a multi-stemmed palm. Cylindrical trunks with a diameter of about 15 cm grow close to each other, are erect and slightly sloping. Green young stems, as they mature, change their color to yellow.

Care:
The optimum temperature for this plant is +22-24 °C throughout the year. At the same time, cooling in winter to +15 °C and warming in summer to +30 °C are quite acceptable. But the palm tree does not tolerate sudden changes in temperature.
