Description

This is a miniature succulent growing in dense groups, forming numerous shoots growing on long stolons submerged in the soil. Plants are almost stemless, forming a rosette of leaves raised above the soil surface.

Care:
Haworthia venosa is unpretentious, grows well and quickly when planted in properly selected soil, stolons produce new, easily rooted rosettes. The beautiful checkerboard pattern and pink-brown color of the leaves appear only in bright sunlight.
