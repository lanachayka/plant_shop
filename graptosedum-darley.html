Description

In appearance, the plants are similar to either graptopetalum or sedum, and the flowering often contrasts with the vegetative part: a sedum-like plant blooms with graptopetalum flowers and vice versa.

Care:
Graptosedum is an unpretentious, undemanding, and well-adapted plant for growing in indoor conditions. It easily tolerates the neighborhood with other succulents but also looks very impressive on its own.
